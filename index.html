About Us

Welcome to Habesha Restaurant, where tradition meets taste. We are proud to serve authentic Eritrean and Ethiopian dishes made with love and the finest ingredients. Our chefs bring generations of cooking knowledge to every plate, offering flavorful stews, fresh injera, and vibrant vegetarian options.

Whether you're craving a spicy zigni, a comforting shiro, or just a warm cup of traditional coffee, our restaurant is the place to be. We’re more than just a dining spot—we’re a cultural experience.

Join us for a meal and feel right at home. At Habesha Restaurant, you're not just a guest—you're family.
